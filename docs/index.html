About Us

We are a tightly-knit team of industry specialists and traders with decades-long exposure to global polymer markets. This has placed us into a position of having supply agreements with both virgin and recycled plastic producers worldwide, especially in East Asia, Europe and North America.

However, the company’s core emphasis lies in the trading and promotion of recycled materials, wherever possible. The truly circular economy may still not be here, but we and our partners do what we can to bring it a notch closer.

Why?

What drives us is the mutual benefit and success of all parties involved in our partnership. This is achieved through transparent trading, flawless supply chain management and consistent competitive results, where each client can have a tailor-made solution that reflects their particular requirement.
