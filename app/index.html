Our Products

Flake

JV Explore offers customers a range of virgin, as well as primarily post-consumer recycled (PCR) and post-industrial flakes. Our super-clean hot washed PET flake is suitable for processing in bottle-to-bottle applications. Other polyethylene products we can provide are PCR and post-industrial rHDPE, as well as post-industrial polypropylene (rPP).

Pellets

JV Explore can also supply polymers in the form of pellets (granules), such as rHDPE, rLDPE and rPP. For each of these products we make sure to provide comprehensive specifications, as well as scientific analysis results and proof of compliance for local legislation.

For more information, such availability, specifications and pricing, please write us at info@jvexplore.com
